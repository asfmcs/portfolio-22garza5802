New York City

What to do

New York City is a place known for all of the tall buildings and the passion for art. There are a lot of museams like the MOMA and the MET, and there are lots of fun musicals every single day on the famous Broadway street. Besides these art related activities, there are a bunch of delicious restaurants all around, varying in prices. Another fun thing to do is walk around the famous central park. If you are not such a fan of walking, they also rent bicycles all around and the park has a very beautiful and relaxing route.

Where to go

Besides the passion for art, there are lots of cool attractions like the famous Statue of Liberty, the Empire State Building, and Rockeffeller Center. If you are a fan of shopping, the famous Fifth Avenue is full of stores both high-end and inexpensive stores with limited edition and exclusive items. Just taking a walk around the city is an experiance in itself and no matter where you are, you are going to have a blast!


Why people love it

NYC is known as the city that never sleeps. People are always out and about, working really hard to achieve their dreams, and most importantly, having fun. Besides this, the scenery is incredible. The center of the city is surrounded by big and beautiful buildings whereas the surroundings have beautiful bodies of water like the Hudson River. It is also home to the famous baseball team, the Yankees, and both the Giants and Jetts who are football teams.

Around the City

NYC is also known for its grid-like streets with the famous Broadway street running diagonally through them all. Central Park is located in the middle of the upper west side and the infamous upper east side. Right below that is Midtown which is home to the Empire State Building and the Chrysler Building. The island is surrounded by the Hudson river and the East river and nevertheless, you can never run out of things to do.


Transportation

In New York, people walk everywhere. Since everythign is so close together, it is very common for people to walk. However if you are going somewhere that is farther away, taking the subway is a very well-known alternative. There are subway stops all around and they are a crucial part of the New York experiance.
